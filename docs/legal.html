Legal Aspects of Online Privacy



When it comes to data privacy and your data online, it seems like there is nobody stopping a company from collecting data and selling it or using it for one reason or another. There are however some laws that exist in order to protect people and especially children from having their privacy breached. However, even with some laws in place to try and protect people, a majority of people are still able to lose their privacy online. The most effective way to maintain privacy online is to be proactive and ensure that you fill in any possible gaps that laws may not cover. Browsing the internet safely and clearing your history and cookies are vital steps to preserve privacy. Another important step to take is to update your anti-virus so that hackers and other bad people are unable to steal your data. Once your data is taken or stolen, it will be out there for good. The legal side of it does provide some form of protection, but a major responsibility lies in every individuals hands too. Making sure that personal information stays personal is important to a majority of people and it should be important to you as well.
